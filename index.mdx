Welcome to the official Lumera Protocol documentation. Lumera is a Cosmos SDK Layer 1 blockchain evolved from Pastel Network, purpose-built for decentralized permanent storage, AI tooling, and seamless cross-chain interoperability. Whether you are running a validator, operating a SuperNode, building applications with the API, or bridging assets across chains, this documentation guides you through every step.

## What You Can Do

Lumera Protocol gives you a full toolkit for building and participating in decentralized infrastructure. Here is a quick overview of what is possible.
